Our Story

Bread baked
the Georgian way

Puri — meaning “bread” in Georgian — brings the ancient craft of Georgian baking to Thornhill, Ontario. Every loaf, pastry, and khachapuri is made fresh daily with tradition, care, and the finest ingredients.

From the crispy golden crust of our Megruli to the molten cheese heart of our Adjaruli, each bite is a journey to the Caucasus.
